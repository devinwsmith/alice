A family business.
In every sense of the phrase.

Five generations.

Thatâ€™s how long the McKissick family has nurtured and grown Alice Manufacturing.

Through wars, a depression, recessions, victories and economic booms, we have always kept our eyes on the road.

We never took anything for granted. And we never let the bad times deter us.

Family businesses are much of what built this great country and the McKissicks have always considered Alice Manufacturing a member of the family.

Which means that every Alice associate who punches a time clock, who manages a department or who works in the office is considered a member of the family.

 

 

Alice Manufacturing Company was founded with five employees in a former brick factory on Main Street.

At Alice Manufacturing, nothing makes us prouder than the fact that our associates stay with us, make a life with us, and feed, clothe, and educate their next generations with what they earn helping Alice move forward.

We have associates who have been with us for decades. Indeed, more than one-quarter of our associates have been with us for at least 25 years. Their experience and loyalty remain invaluable to us.

And we have younger generations who come through our doors everyday, bringing with them a technological and business savvy that fuel our confidence about the future.

Alice Manufacturing is both a family business, and a family. That might be the only part of us that feels a little old-fashioned.

But we have no intention of ever changing that.
