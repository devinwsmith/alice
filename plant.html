Our plant has been growing,
well, like a plant.

At Alice Manufacturing, our business has maintained a healthy heartbeat because of our willingness to adapt to the times, to advance with technology and to have the foresight to anticipate business trends.

Nowhere is this reflected more than in our actual plant.

Ours is the largest wide width fabric manufacturing facility in North America, producing an array of cotton and poly/cotton greige goods in widths ranging up to 140" (3600mm).

We operate under Lean and Six Sigma principles, which has enabled us to perfectly marry efficiency with high quality.

We invite you to view these photographs of our plant in Easley, South Carolina.

Outstanding products +
Outstanding customer service =
Outstanding results

 

Those of us at Alice Manufacturing understand that the easy way of doing things is almost always more difficult in the end.

So we don’t cut corners. We don’t scrimp on quality. We never relegate you to a junior assistant or an endless stream of voicemails.

Well-produced products matter. And so does customer service.

We specialize in manufacturing greige fabrics of outstanding quality. From here, our customers can finish the fabric in any way that benefits their business.

Beyond that, we make specific products for the health care industry, the US government and many apparel companies.

Additionally, we are always open to discussions regarding customized products. Please feel free to inquire.
